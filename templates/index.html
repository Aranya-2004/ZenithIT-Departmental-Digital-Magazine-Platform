About TechPulse

About the Magazine

TechPulse is the official annual magazine of the Department of Information Technology, KGEC. Launched in 2020, it serves as a vibrant platform to showcase technical brilliance, research, innovation, and the creative spirit of IT students. From cutting-edge algorithms to real-world projects, every edition celebrates the journey of turning ideas into impact.

Spirit of TechPulse

We believe in curiosity over conformity, code over chaos, and collaboration over competition. TechPulse is more than a magazine — it’s a movement to inspire, connect, and empower the next generation of tech leaders. Every page reflects passion, resilience, and the unbreakable IT spirit of KGEC.
