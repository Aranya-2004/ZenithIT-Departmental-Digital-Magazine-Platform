About ZenITh

About the Magazine

ZenITh is the first-ever official magazine launched by the Department of Information Technology in May 2026. It stands as a symbol of creativity, innovation, and technical excellence among IT students. The magazine serves as a dynamic platform for showcasing ideas, articless, achievements, and emerging technologies. From insightful articles to visionary concepts, ZenITh reflects the evolving tech culture of the department. More than just a publication, it celebrates the spirit of learning, collaboration, and the journey of turning imagination into reality.

Spirit of ZenITh

We believe in curiosity over conformity, vision over limitation, and collaboration over competition. ZenITh is not merely a magazine — it is a reflection of the ideas, ambition, and creative spirit that define the Department of Information Technology at KGEC. Crafted through innovation and driven by passion, every page captures stories of resilience, imagination, and the pursuit of excellence. More than a publication, ZenITh stands as a timeless expression of the evolving IT culture and the voices shaping its future.
